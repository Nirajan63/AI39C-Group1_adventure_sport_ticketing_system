Our Story

Rooted in Kathmandu,
Built for Adventure

Adventure was founded by a team of passionate explorers in Kathmandu — one of Nepal's most iconic gateways to the Himalayas. We believe the mountain kingdom holds some of the world's most exhilarating experiences, and we share them responsibly.

Every trip is designed with deep local knowledge, certified safety standards, and genuine care for both our clients and the environment we operate in.

  • 📍 Thamel, Kathmandu, Bagmati Province, Nepal
  • 🛡 Government-licensed guides & certified safety equipment
  • 🌿 Eco-conscious packages for sustainable adventure tourism
