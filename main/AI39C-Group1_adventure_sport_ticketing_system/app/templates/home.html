Our Story

Rooted in Kathmandu,
Built for Adventure

Thrill Sphere was founded by a team of passionate explorers in Kathmandu — one of the world's most iconic gateways to the high Himalayas. We believe the mountain kingdom holds some of the world's most exhilarating adventures, and we aim to share them securely.

Every trip is engineered with deep local expertise, certified safety gear, and genuine support for both our guests and the environment we explore in.

  • 📍 Thamel, Kathmandu, Bagmati Province, Nepal
  • 🛡 Government-licensed guides & certified safety protocols
  • 🌿 Eco-conscious booking models for sustainable tourism
